20. MultiCast - Share values, not side effects Subjects allow you broadcast values from a shared source, while limiting side effects to only one occurance. You start with a regular Observable, then multicast it to a Subject to be consumed by the end user. This magic happens because a single shared subscription is created to the underlying observable.
